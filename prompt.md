The platform must solve the problem of poor engagement and low retention found in traditional written documentation.  2. The Core "Candy Crush" LogicSaga Map Progression: Create a visual, non-linear path of levels (Levels 1–14) . Reward & Feedback LoopInstant Feedback: Every action must trigger a UI overlay explaining why the choice was correct or unsafe.
