Product Requirements Document (PRD): SOP Quest – Next Gen1. Executive SummarySOP Quest is a web-based, gamified training platform designed to transform static, written Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) into an interactive, "Candy Crush" style learning experience. By moving from a "watch-only" model to a "think and choose" simulation, the platform aims to solve the industry-wide problem of low engagement and poor knowledge retention in safety-critical roles.  2. Problem StatementCurrent SOP implementation suffers from several critical trends:  Lack of Reference: Employees rarely refer to existing SOPs during actual work.  Format Issues: Documentation focuses on "What" rather than "How" and is often a "One-Size-Fits-All" approach.  Low Engagement: Written formats fail to sustain attention, leading to deviations from standard processes and increased safety risks.  Limited Accessibility: Procedures are difficult to access at the point of work.  3. Target AudiencePrimary: Operational employees performing daily metering activities (e.g., Single Phase Teams).  Secondary: Training managers for induction and refresher training, and safety auditors.  4. Functional Requirements4.1. Core Gameplay Mechanics (The "Candy Crush" Loop)Saga Map Progression: A visual quest map consisting of sequential levels.  Level Locking: Users must complete prerequisite levels (e.g., Foundation and Essentials) before unlocking Operational or Expert levels.  Star/Score System: Users earn points for correct answers (+) and lose points (-) for wrong ones.  Three-Star Rating: A 3-star system to encourage re-playability and mastery.4.2. Level ArchetypesLevel TypeGame Style Purpose KnowledgeQuiz-BasedCompliance, Safety, and Audit Readiness.OperationalSimulation/Scenario-BasedHands-on practice for operations and hazard handling.TechnicalInteractive ChecklistMastering specific technical sequences and efficiency.4.3. Simulation Features (Three.js/R3F)Hazard Identification: 3D scenarios where users must identify unsafe conditions like busbar hazards or reptiles.  Interaction Tasks: Drag-and-drop or click actions for tasks such as wearing PPE, checking for leakages, or routing cables.  Decision Points: Branching paths where users choose the next safe step in a procedure.  4.4. Feedback & AssessmentInstant Feedback: Immediate "Right/Wrong" alerts following an action.  Instructional Correction: Explanations provided after every action to reinforce safety behaviors.  Final Certification: A score-based digital certificate upon completing the full quest (e.g., 14 levels).  5. Non-Functional RequirementsVisual Aesthetic: "Apple-level" UI—modern, cinematic, and premium—using a dark-mode theme.  Performance: Fast loading of 3D assets (.glb/.gltf) to maintain engagement.Scalability: Architecture must allow for the easy addition of the remaining 13 SOP modules.  6. User Journey (MVP: Feeder Pillar Quest)Login: User authenticates via Firebase.Dashboard: User views their "SOP Champion" rank and XP.  Map: User enters the "Feeder Pillar" quest and clicks Level-1: Prework Safety.  Gameplay: User performs a 3D reptile check (Level-4) and safe door opening (Level-5).  Reward: User earns the "Scenario Master" badge after Level-10.  Completion: Final Assessment is cleared, and score is updated on the global leaderboard.  7. Success MetricsCompletion Rate: % of employees who finish the entire 14-level quest.Safety Proficiency: Reduction in "Wrong Answer" alerts during repeated simulations.Engagement: Frequency of users returning to the app for "Practice Mode."
